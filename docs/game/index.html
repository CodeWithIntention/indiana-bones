Indian Bones (IB, as we call him) is a bastard spawned from a torid love triangle between a Portuguese Jesuit, an Amazon witch, and the Church.

He possesses the power of immortality (though limited), and is prone to busts of rage when he drinks water. While enraged, he is invincible and has an insatiable appitite.

He is a freelance archaeologist and adventurer insearch of ancient artifacts for the highest bidder. He supplements his income by selling furry animals to the Hong Kong wet markets.

He hates spiders and anything that has venom because getting bit or stung by one will kill him.
