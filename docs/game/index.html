The Story of Indiana Bones

Indiana Bones (IB, as we call him) is a bastard spawned from a torrid love triangle between a Portuguese Jesuit, an Amazon witch, and the Church.

He possesses the power of immortality (though limited), and is consumed with rage when he drinks water. While enraged, he is invincible and has an insatiable appetite.

IB is a freelance archaeologist and adventurer in search of ancient artifacts for the highest bidder. He supplements his income by selling furry animals to the Hong Kong wet markets.

He hates spiders and anything that has venom because getting bit or stung by one will kill him.
